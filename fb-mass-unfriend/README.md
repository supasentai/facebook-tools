### How to use Mass Unfriend

1. Go to your official Facebook profile friend list:
   `https://www.facebook.com/me/friends` (Ensure you are logged in).
2. Scroll down to load the segment of friends you wish to review or bulk-remove.
3. Open **Developer Tools** (`F12`) -> **Console** tab.
4. Copy the entire contents of `fb-mass-unfriend.js`, paste it into the console, and press **Enter**.

> ⚠️ **Warning:** Unfriending too many accounts within a short period can flag Facebook's automation detection. It is highly recommended to limit this tool to **50-100 unfriendings per session**.
