### How to use Newsfeed Cleaner (AdBlock)

1. Go to your Facebook homepage / Newsfeed: `https://www.facebook.com/`
2. Open **Developer Tools** (`F12`) -> **Console** tab.
3. Copy the entire contents of `fb-newsfeed-cleaner.js`, paste it into the console, and press **Enter**.
4. Enjoy a clean, ad-free scrolling experience in real-time.
